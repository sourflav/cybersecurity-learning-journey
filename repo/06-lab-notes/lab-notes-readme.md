# Lab Notes

This folder contains notes related to hands on exercises, practice sessions, and technical experiments.

It is meant to document practical work in a simple and structured way.

## What this folder should contain

- lab summaries
- step by step notes
- commands used during exercises
- errors encountered
- corrections and lessons learned
- simple explanations of what was tested and why

## Goal

The goal of this section is to keep a practical record of learning activities and to turn hands on experience into material that can be reviewed and improved over time.
